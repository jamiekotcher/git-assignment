When I graduated High School, my family traveled to Germany and Italy. In Germany, we visited Munich, the hometown of some of our old neighbors. We toured our first biergarten and saw Neuschwanstein Castle

In Italy, we saw Venice and Rome. Venice was my favorite since the canals were something I had never seen before!

August 2016: Vancouver

In August I traveled without my family or a school for the first time out of the country. My friend and I went to Vancouver, Canada. I loved Vancouver for its ability to contain both a city and a nature escape. We hiked Grouse Grind, which is one of the hardest things I’ve ever done!

May-June 2017: South Africa

This coming summer, I will be going on my second dialogue -- this time to South Africa! We will be there for 3.5 weeks, working with local entrepreneurs and a nearby business school. I’m so excited to explore a new continent!!
